About Me

I’ve always had a curiosity in how things work growing up, so I went and got myself a degree in engineering. As a biological systems engineer, I'm a bit more versatile than other engineers because I have knowledge in a variety of subjects ranging from electrical circuits to chemistry. I’m not limited to focusing on one specific subject matter like a chemical engineer or an electrical engineer. I have a experience in the lab setting and as well as process design. You could ask me to plan the production of a product from start to finish, while taking in account various factors like cost and materials.

We live in the age of the internet, and many industries, not just biotech, are becoming more technologically advanced and internet-dependent. I have a strong interest in the biotech industry. It’s a growing industry, but many companies still rely on old technology and are still operator dependent. I’ve recently added web development and design to my skillset. As an engineer and a full-stack developer, I want to help usher the biotech industry into using more modern technology. Many companies will eventually move towards automation and web-based systems, and I’ll be ready for it when it happens.
